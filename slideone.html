WHO IS ANTONI MUNTADAS?

Antoni Muntadas is a multimedia artist from Barcelona, who has put together meaningful and important works of art, public art projects, and videotapes. Antoni center his art around critiquing the media such as television, the press, advertising, & both public and invisible telecommunication networks basically anything with a voice. As stated on https://anthology.rhizome.org/ Antoni is "Deconstructing the systems of representation and information that pervade the contemporary media landscape, Muntadas posits a compelling discourse on the invisible mechanisms that inform the production and reception of mass media texts."
