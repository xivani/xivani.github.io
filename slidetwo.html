What Is The File Room?

The File Room is a project that bring forwar and addresses censorship. It is a interactive open database that has records of past cases of censorship around the world. Antoni and his team working on the case was unable to complete his original goal of reintroducing deleted and supressed materials back into public records.

So instead visitors of the site are welcomed from all over the world to add there own experiences of both artistic and cultural censorship to an open-ended archive. The file Room is sculpted by the visitors and there experiences. It shapes an evolving archive of how supression of information around the world is orchestrated throughout history in different contexts, countries, and civilizations.

According to MUNTADAS AND RANDOLPH STREET GALLERY, 1994 “THE FILE ROOM, RATHER THAN BEING PRESENTED AS A FINISHED WORK, IS... AN OPEN SYSTEM THAT BECOMES ACTIVATED, ‘FILED’ AND DEVELOPED THROUGH THE PUBLIC PROCESS OF ITS OWN EXISTENCE..”.
